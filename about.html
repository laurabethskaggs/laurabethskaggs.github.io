About Me

I'm Laura Skaggs Bowling — a project manager, business analyst, and AI practitioner based in Baton Rouge. I lead complex modernization efforts in state government, blending deep domain knowledge with a hands-on technical approach.

Over the past six years, I’ve led large-scale initiatives across licensing, education, case management, and financial systems. My sweet spot is turning ambiguity into action: translating regulatory requirements into working software, aligning technical and business teams, and managing delivery from roadmap to rollout.

My AI work spans training LLMs, designing conversational agents, auditing model outputs, and guiding prompt engineering strategy. I bring structure to experimentation — whether it’s refining task quality, developing evaluation rubrics, or validating model behavior in real-world use cases.

I work fluently across HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL, and bring a systems-thinking mindset to every project. Whether it’s mapping legacy workflows, optimizing user experience, or analyzing data patterns, I focus on building solutions that make impact obvious.

When I’m not solving problems with tech, I’m probably reading about them — from AI policy to plant physiology. I believe the best systems are built by the most curious minds. Let’s connect.
